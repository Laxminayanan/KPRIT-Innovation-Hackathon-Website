Innovation Hackathon

24 Hours. One Prototype. Unlimited Impact.

Event Objective

The Innovation Hackathon aims to challenge participants to design and build innovative solutions to real-world problems within 24 hours. This event encourages creativity, technical excellence, collaboration, and rapid problem-solving.


Participants will demonstrate their ability to ideate, prototype, and present impactful solutions under time constraints. The event promotes innovation-driven thinking and practical implementation skills.

Team Formation

  • Team Size: Minimum 2 and Maximum 4 members.
  • Individual participation is not allowed.
  • Open to all departments and all years.
  • Cross-department teams are allowed.
  • Cross-college participation is not allowed.
  • Limited to 30 teams (first come, first served).
  • No team member substitution after registration closes.
